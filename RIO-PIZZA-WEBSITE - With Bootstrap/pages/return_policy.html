Return Policy

Our Commitment

At RIO PIZZA, we want you to be 100% satisfied with your order. If you encounter any issues, here’s what you need to know about our return and refund policy.

Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns or refunds if your order is incorrect, damaged, or fails to meet our quality standards. Please notify us within 24 hours of delivery.

How to Request

Contact our support team at support@gmail.com or call +970 599 000 000 with your order details to initiate a return.

Process & Timeline

After verifying your request, we will offer a replacement, refund, or credit towards your next order. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

Non-returnable Items

For safety and hygiene reasons, consumed or improperly stored food items are not eligible for returns.
